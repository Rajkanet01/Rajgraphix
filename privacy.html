Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 15, 2025

1. Introduction

At Rajgraphix, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and messages provided through our contact form.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our website, such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide services.
  • Improve our website and services.
  • Communicate with you about updates or promotions (with your consent).

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • With service providers (e.g., Formspree for contact form submissions) who assist us in operating our website.
  • When required by law or to protect our rights.

5. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance user experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect website functionality.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the Contact section.
