About Me

I’m Raj Kanet, a passionate graphic designer and UI/UX designer, dedicated to creating visually stunning and user-friendly designs. I specialize in branding, social media content, and interface design, ensuring that every project I work on stands out with creativity and impact.

I also manage RajGraphix on Instagram, where I showcase my latest design projects, creative experiments, and design tips. In addition, I run my YouTube channel, Raj Kanet, where I share entertaining vlogs and comedy content.

With a strong eye for aesthetics and a commitment to innovation, I constantly explore new design trends and techniques to refine my skills. Let’s connect and create something extraordinary!
